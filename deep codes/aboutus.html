About Us

Our Deep Fake Detection Project

Welcome to our Deep Fake Detection platform, a powerful tool designed to combat the rise of fake media. Our solution utilizes cutting-edge deep learning technologies, including Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) and ResNet models, to analyze videos and images for signs of manipulation. With this platform, users can easily detect deep fakes with high accuracy, ensuring authenticity and trust in the digital world.

How We Developed It

Our development process combined LSTM for sequence-based data analysis and ResNet, a robust convolutional neural network, for image recognition. This hybrid deep learning model allows for the detection of both subtle and complex manipulations, giving our tool a strong edge over traditional detection methods.
