Our Services

We offer a range of advanced services to detect and combat deepfake content. Our solutions utilize the latest in AI technology to ensure accuracy, speed, and reliability.

1. Deepfake Video Detection

Using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), our system analyzes video frames to detect tampered or altered content in real time.

2. Audio-Visual Synchronization Analysis

We utilize advanced models to ensure that audio tracks and visual elements are in sync. This method is crucial for detecting audio-visual manipulation in deepfake videos.

3. Frequency Domain Analysis

We employ frequency domain techniques to detect subtle tampering in videos that may not be noticeable to the naked eye, ensuring more accurate detection of sophisticated deepfakes.

4. Temporal Consistency Checks

By leveraging Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, we assess temporal consistency in video sequences, helping to detect unnatural transitions and manipulations over time.

5. Automated Forensic Reporting

Our platform generates detailed forensic reports that highlight detected deepfakes, providing clear evidence for further investigation or legal action.
